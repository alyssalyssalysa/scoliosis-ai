ScoliosisAI

ScoliosisAI is a student-led organization founded to make convenient early detection through AI and community support.

The ideal time for scoliosis correction is before puberty. However, several of our team members were diagnosed in their late teenage years, resulting in the need for more intensive treatment.

To ensure other teenagers, especially those in under-resourced communities, do not experience the same delays, we strive to make detection of scoliosis easily accessible.

What is Scoliosis?

Scoliosis is a lateral curvature of the spine. Globally, approximately 3% of adolescents have idiopathic scoliosis, a condition with no known cause.

That is approximately the entire population of the United States!

Early detection is often missed due to the inconvenience of X-rays or the limited availability of screening equipment and programs.

Our Solution

Make early detection of scoliosis accessible and free of specialized hardware:

1) Capture an image of the subject’s bare back safely and anonymously using the smartphone camera.
2) Utilize a digital scoliometer with the smartphone’s gyroscope and accelerometer to measure spine angle.
3) Provide resources and community support on early detection, diagnosis, and treatment.
